Covid-19 Dashboard

This is the Covid-19 dashboard. The data is extracted from Worldometers as at 26 July 2021, 2pm Malaysia time. This interactive dashboard will display the Total cases, Active cases, Total death, Total recovered and Serious cases of Covid-19 worldwide. The information projected from the dashboard can use as awarness to citizen and travelers of the seriousness of Covid-19 cases in respective country. This dashboard can be further enhanced with additional data if it is available for public.
